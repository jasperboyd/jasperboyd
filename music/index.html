Current Projects

Windy Tunnel Media

Windy Tunnel Media is a DIY record label that I started working on in the spring of 2013. My goal with the project was to bring musicians together under one banner in the spirit of cooperation and collaboration. You can see the result here.

The Weather Sound

The Weather Sound is a experiment in computer music. The premise was to create a program that made ambient music from real time weather data that didn't just sound like a smelly heap of garbage. The project is built in MAX / MSP.

The Purest Cacophony

The Purest Cacophony is my solo music venture. After playing the supporting role in a number of different groups I set out to make music that I could call my own. With the help of Jonathan Dana, Mike Tierney and Paul Puiia, the group released it's first EP I Will Not Fall on June 12th, 2013. You can find out more at The Purest Cacophony's Website.
