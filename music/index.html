Ongoing Endeavors

THE PUREST CACOPHONY

The Purest Cacophony is my solo venture. For a long time I was the bassist in a number of different groups and after "taking orders" for what felt like a long time I wanted to do something where creatively I was in control.

The Purest Cacophony's debut EP, I Will Not Fall, was released in June of 2013 and more is on the way.

THE HALFS

The Halfs is the ongoing songwriting collaboration of myself and Nina Petropolous. We work together on all aspects of the sound which leads to a unique voice and character to the music.

The Halfs' debut EP, Shut Off The Fan!, was released in April of 2013 and a full-length album is scheduled to be released in late summer.
