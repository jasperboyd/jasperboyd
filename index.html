Current Projects

WINDY TUNNEL MEDIA

Inspired by David Byrne's "How Music Works" I decided to start my own DIY record label. Not only to collaborate with other musicians, but also to practice my web design skills. You can see the progress here.

THE WEATHER SOUND

As part of Senior Tutorial in Computer Music (MUSC270) I created a MAX/MSP patch that uses real time weather data from Wunderground and creates ambient music from it. In addition I used the Jitter library to create a visualizer that accompanies the music as part of Computer Graphics (CSCI 260). You can listen here.

I Will Not Fall

LISTEN TO & DOWNLOAD MY NEW EP FOR FREE!

I Will Not Fall was written & recorded in the winter of 2012-2013. All the recording was done myself, as well as the instruments with the exception of trumpet and drums, which were performed by Theo Van Dyke and Jonathan Dana. The EP was mastered by Mike Tierney and the album artwork is by Paul Puiia. You can download it here.
